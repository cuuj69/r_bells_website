Academic Levels
Creche Level: Our creche program provides a safe and nurturing environment for our youngest learners, focusing on early development and socialization. Kindergarten Level: Our kindergarten program encourages curiosity and creativity through play-based learning, preparing students for their academic journey. Primary Level: In our primary program, we provide a comprehensive curriculum that promotes critical thinking, problem-solving, and a love for learning. Junior High School Level: At the junior high school level, we offer a challenging curriculum that prepares students for high school through rigorous academics and independent thinking. At Ringing Bells Preparatory School, we prioritize individualized attention, open communication with parents, and a supportive learning community across all academic levels.
Activities
At Ringing Bells Preparatory School, we believe in providing a well-rounded education that extends beyond the classroom. We offer a range of activities to enrich our students' learning experience and promote their personal growth. Here are some of the activities we provide: Sports and Physical Education: Our sports program encourages physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship. Students can participate in team sports like soccer, basketball, and cricket, as well as individual activities like swimming and athletics. Arts and Creative Expression: Through our arts program, students explore various artistic disciplines, including visual arts, music, and performing arts. They have the opportunity to engage in painting, drawing, sculpture, music, dance, and drama. Field Trips and Educational Excursions: Our school organizes field trips and educational excursions to complement classroom learning. Students visit museums, historical sites, science centers, and nature reserves.
Facilities
At Ringing Bells Preparatory School, we strive to provide a conducive learning environment equipped with modern facilities to support our students' academic and personal growth. Here are some of the facilities we offer: Well-Equipped Classrooms: Our classrooms are designed to facilitate interactive and engaging learning experiences, equipped with modern teaching aids and technology. Science and Computer Labs: We have dedicated science and computer labs where students can conduct experiments, explore scientific concepts, and develop their digital literacy skills. Library: Our school library houses a wide range of books, references, and resources to foster a love for reading, research, and independent learning. Sports Facilities: We have well-maintained sports facilities that allow students to participate in various physical activities, promoting fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship. Who We Are

Welcome to Ringing Bells Preparatory School, where we lay the foundation for lifelong learning and success. As an esteemed educational institution, we offer a comprehensive program for students from Nursery to Junior High School, providing them with a nurturing and enriching environment to thrive academically, emotionally, and socially.
At Ringing Bells Preparatory School, we believe in fostering a love for learning from the very beginning. Our dedicated team of experienced educators is passionate about guiding each child's educational journey, ensuring that they develop a strong academic foundation while also nurturing their individual interests and talents. With a focus on holistic development, we empower our students to become critical thinkers, effective communicators, and responsible global citizens.
